# 💊 PaisaTracker — Smart Financial Tracker for Pharmacy Owners

## 🚀 Overview

**PaisaTracker** is an intelligent financial and inventory management app designed specifically for small pharmacy owners. It eliminates the chaos of managing cash sales, UPI payments, supplier bills, and udhaar (credit) records across multiple registers and apps.

With automation, AI, and real-time insights, PaisaTracker ensures that shopkeepers always know their **actual daily earnings** and financial status.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Small pharmacy owners often:

* Maintain **cash sales manually**
* Track **UPI payments via SMS/apps**
* Record **udhaar (credit) in notebooks**
* Handle **supplier bills separately**

This leads to:

* ❌ Financial confusion
* ❌ Missed payments
* ❌ Stock mismanagement
* ❌ No clear profit visibility

---

## 💡 Solution

PaisaTracker unifies everything into a **single smart platform** that:

* Tracks all payment modes automatically
* Digitizes handwritten udhaar records
* Monitors inventory through bills
* Provides real-time earnings insights

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📲 1. UPI SMS Reader

* Automatically reads UPI transaction SMS
* Categorizes income and expenses
* Eliminates manual entry

### 📷 2. Udhaar Scanner (OCR)

* Scans handwritten credit records
* Converts them into structured digital entries
* Tracks customer dues

### 📦 3. Smart Inventory Tracking

* Upload supplier bills
* Auto-update medicine stock
* Alerts for low inventory

### 💰 4. Real-Time Earnings Dashboard

* Shows **today’s actual profit**
* Combines:

  * Cash sales
  * UPI payments
  * Credit transactions
  * Expenses

### ⚠️ 5. Cash Shortage Alerts

* Predicts cash flow issues
* Warns before shortages happen

### 🔔 6. Payment Reminders

* Automatically reminds customers via:

  * WhatsApp
  * SMS
* Reduces pending udhaar
